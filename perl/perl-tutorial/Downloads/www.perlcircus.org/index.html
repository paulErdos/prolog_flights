Welcome. Perl Circus has been serving up fresh buttered Perl tricks since 2001. During that time our code snippets have delighted programmers of all ages. But the life of the Perl coder isn't all fun and games. A good Perl programmer has a skill set somewhere between a hacker and trainer of wild animals. Camels can be dangerous when cornered.

I have a small bit of experience with the rough and tumble life of a Perl programmer and that's what this site is about. These are just a few tricks I've collected over the years. Enjoy.
